FAQs
How do I save an artwork?
Tap the bookmark icon on any artwork card on the map or detail page. You must be signed in to save artworks. All saved artworks appear in your Saved tab.
What is a feeling log?
A feeling log lets you record your emotional response to an artwork — choosing from a set of feelings, adding extra emojis, and leaving a note. Logs are private by default unless you choose to share them publicly.
How do I create an art walk?
Go to the Walks tab and tap "+ New walk". Add a name, description, and at least 2 artwork stops from the search. Choose whether your walk is public or private, then tap Create Walk.
How do I add a new artwork?
Tap the + (Add) tab. First search to check if the artwork already exists. If not, a form appears — fill in the title, artist, description, category, location, and optionally upload photos, then tap Pin Installation.
Can I delete my data?
Yes. Go to Profile → Privacy → Delete account to permanently remove all your data. You can also delete individual feeling logs inside the Log Feeling sheet.
